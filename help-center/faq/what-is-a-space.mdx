## Overview

A Space in Horuse is your project's dedicated workspace.

- Organize documentation by project, team, or client
- Each Space has its own pages and structure
- You can invite collaborators to specific Spaces
- Permissions and visibility are managed per Space
- Use Spaces to separate unrelated docs

Spaces help keep your work organized and accessible for every use case.
