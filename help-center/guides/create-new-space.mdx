## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'New Space'">
    In the sidebar, click the <b>New Space</b> button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter details">
    Fill in the Space name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Space">
    Click <b>Create</b>. Your new Space appears in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators (optional)">
    Add teammates via email, or skip for later.
  </Step>
</Steps>
